Coops

Coops is a young rising star in the hip hop and rap scene, captivating audiences with his unique style. Born in San Fransisco and raised in Lawrence, Kansas, Coops discovered his passion for music at an early age, writing his first bars when he was just a kid. As he grew up, Coops continued to hone his craft, drawing inspiration from artists like Isaiah Rashad, Smino, Benny The Butcher, Roc Marciano, Nas, and many other notable artists. His music shows versatility with vibey dynamics, high energy hip-hop, and methodical rap with deep bass and bouncy beats. He speaks about his life and draws listeners into a refreshing vision while keeping them on the edge of their seats. Keep your ears open and pay attention because if you don’t, you might miss something big…

About Us

Murphy Music is a music streaming management and consultation service that focuses on providing the business side of music operations so artists can focus on expression. Through marketing, financial support, branding, and networking, Murphy Music provides emerging artists the opportunity all musicians crave and seeks to make dreams come true.

Alec Murphy is the founder born and raised in Lawrence, Kansas. He has had a passion for music from a young age and now has converted that passion into creating a much needed service in the music industry. With an artist friendly approach, he is determined to provide support and empower new talents. It is a core value to maintain artists creative freedom and give them control over their art.
